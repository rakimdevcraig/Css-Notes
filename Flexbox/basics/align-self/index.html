Align self:allows you to override the align items property. In this chat interface we have the items all aligned to the left or flex-start. But if we want to move specific items to the right we can override align-items with align-self

  1. Can you get me a big salad?
  2. What big salad? I'm going to the coffee shop.
  3. They have big salads.
  4. I've never seen a big salad.
  5. They have a big salad.
  6. Is that what I ask for?
  7. The BIG salad?

With Align-self on the received message we get to override align-items:flex-start and move them out of the natural flow to the right with align-self:flex-end
