Margin Collapse

Rule 1:Only vertical margins collapse

Paragraph one with 24px margin top and bottom

Paragraph two with 24px margin top and bottom

Instead of these 2 paragraphs having 48px inbetween them they only have 24 because of margin collapse (I can check with the inspector)

Rule2:Only adjacent elements collapse

Paragraph one with 24px margin top and bottom


Paragraph two with 24px margin top and bottom

We put a "br" element between these 2 paragraphs and that cancels out the collapse as you can see there's more space between the same 2 paragraphs in this example compared to the one above

Rule 3:The bigger margin wins

Paragraph one with 24px margin top and bottom

Paragraph two with 72px margin top and bottom

there will be 72px between these two elements because the bottom element has a margin of 72 so that will cancel out the 24 and collapse
